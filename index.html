Crystal Collector

How to play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add specific amount of points to your total score. You will win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score is more than the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the crystals will have a new value
